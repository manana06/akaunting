# Akaunting Localization for Greek SMEs 🇬🇷
**Module:** SWE6005 - Open Source Software Development  
**University:** University of Bolton

## 1. Project Overview
This project focuses on the localization and adaptation of the **Akaunting** open-source platform to meet the specific financial and accounting needs of Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs) in Greece. Our goal is to reduce operational costs and ensure data sovereignty through a self-hosted open-source solution.

## 3. Business Problem 
Greek SMEs often face:
* High licensing costs for proprietary ERP systems.
* Poor localization (translation/terminology) in international software.
* Complexity in complying with local accounting standards.

## 4. Proposed Solution
We are contributing to the Akaunting project by:
* **Refactoring Localization:** Improving Greek translations in `general.php` and `invoices.php`.
* **Standardization:** Adapting invoicing logic for Greek business requirements.
* **Documentation:** Providing a wiki-based guide for implementation.
